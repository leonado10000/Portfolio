Community Support

Everything your school needs.

Designed to reduce administrative overhead so educators can focus on what matters most: teaching.

Comprehensive Student Profiles

Keep track of demographics, medical records, disciplinary history, and academic performance in one unified, secure profile for every student.

Smart Attendance

Lightning-fast daily attendance logging for teachers. Automatic alert generation for parents when students are marked absent.

Gradebook & Transcripts

Customizable grading scales. Easily generate term report cards, calculate GPAs, and print official transcripts with a single click.

Self-Hosted & Secure

Your data belongs to you. SchoolMate is built to be self-hosted on your institution's servers, ensuring absolute compliance with local data privacy laws (FERPA/GDPR).

Core Modules

A modular approach means you only use what you need. Turn features on or off based on your school's unique requirements.

Admin Portal

Manage staff roles, school calendar, system settings, and global announcements.

Teacher Dashboard

Quick access to assigned classes, grading interfaces, attendance sheets, and lesson planning.

Fee Management

Track tuition payments, generate invoices, manage discounts, and send automated payment reminders.

Parent Portal

Secure login for parents to view their child's progress, attendance, and communicate with teachers.
